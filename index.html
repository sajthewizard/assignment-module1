Why Your help is Required?

Monsoon storms and incessant rains have put Bangladesh in the face of devastating floods which led to deaths of at least 32 people. More than nine million people are homeless as floodwaters inundate their homes destroying their belongings. The state minister for disaster management and relief Enamur Rahman said heavy rains in Meghalaya and Assam caused the massive floods in Bangladesh. He said these are the worst floods to hit the districts of Sylhet and Sunamganj in 122 years.
